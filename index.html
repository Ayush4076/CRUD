About The AECD Forum

AECD Forum has global members including the European, American, Australian, African and Asian business communities that are in synergy with your sector and looking for potential investments & growth.

Our aim is to educate the entrepreneur on the legal and regulatory requirements, offering support to the company, firms, and individuals at every stage to make sure they are compliant and continually growing.
